Biography

Lihan Hu is a Ph.D. student at The University of Iowa, advised by Dr. Peng Jiang. His research interests include High Performance Computing, Machine Learning and Image Processing. Before joined in The University of Iowa, he obtained his Master’s degree at Hohai University and advised by Prof. Lixin Han. He received his Bachelor’s degree at Nanjing Audit University.
